Nagarjuna

Nagarjuna is one of Bangalore’s most loved spots for authentic Andhra cuisine. Famous for its spicy flavors and hearty meals, the restaurant is best known for its iconic Andhra Meals served on banana leaves, along with dishes like Chicken Nagarjuna and Mutton Fry.

The restaurant chain has several outlets in Bangalore, with the Residency Road location being the most iconic. It’s a go-to place for spice lovers and anyone looking to explore traditional Andhra fare in a comfortable and bustling setting.
