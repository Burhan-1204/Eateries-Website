Airlines Hotel

Airlines Hotel is a legendary open-air eatery nestled under a canopy of large trees in the heart of Bangalore. A popular spot for generations, it is best known for its calm ambiance, retro charm, and South Indian breakfast items like dosas, idlis, and vadas and its famous Delhi wale Chole Bature.

Located on Lavelle Road, it offers a nostalgic experience with outdoor seating where waiters serve you in your car or at shaded tables. The filter coffee here is highly recommended, making it a beloved destination for locals and tourists alike.
