MTR (Mavalli Tiffin Room)

Mavalli Tiffin Room, popularly known as MTR, is a legendary South Indian vegetarian restaurant in Bangalore, established in 1924. Located near Lalbagh, it is renowned for its authentic Karnataka-style meals and classic dosas.

MTR’s legacy spans decades of serving high-quality food with a focus on hygiene and traditional recipes. It is also known for innovating the idea of Rava Idli during World War II. The full meals offered here, served on stainless steel plates, are a treat for food connoisseurs.
