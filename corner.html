Corner House

Corner House is a legendary dessert parlor in Bangalore, renowned for its iconic ice cream sundaes and nostalgic charm. Established in the early 1980s, it has become a household name for generations of Bangaloreans seeking comfort in cold desserts.

Famous for its signature *Death by Chocolate*, Corner House offers a wide variety of sundaes, milkshakes, and fruit desserts. The outlets maintain a simple yet welcoming ambiance, with the most popular one located in Residency Road.
