Truffles

Truffles is a beloved casual dining restaurant chain in Bangalore, famous for its American-style menu and youthful vibe. Known for juicy burgers, creamy pastas, and indulgent desserts, Truffles has become a go-to spot for college students and young professionals.

The cafe-style interiors, quick service, and affordable pricing make it a top choice for hangouts and casual meals. The most popular outlet is located on St. Marks Road, attracting large crowds daily with its signature burgers and shakes.
