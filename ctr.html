CTR (Central Tiffin Room)

Central Tiffin Room, popularly known as CTR, is one of the most iconic South Indian restaurants in Bangalore. Established in the 1920s, it has retained its charm and continues to attract food lovers from across the city. Known for its crispy, buttery dosas and strong filter coffee, CTR is a haven for breakfast and tiffin enthusiasts.

The eatery is located in the Malleshwaram area and is famous for maintaining its traditional ambiance and taste over the decades. The Benne Masala Dosa (Butter Masala Dosa) is especially a favorite among locals and tourists alike.
