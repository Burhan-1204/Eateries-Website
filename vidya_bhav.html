Vidyarthi Bhavan

Vidyarthi Bhavan, located in the bustling Gandhi Bazaar of Bangalore, is a legendary vegetarian restaurant established in 1943. It is a heritage eatery famed for its unique take on South Indian tiffin items, especially its thick, crispy Masala Dosa that has become iconic among locals and tourists alike.

The restaurant has retained its old-world charm with a retro ambiance and minimalistic interiors, drawing large crowds daily. Vidyarthi Bhavan's dosas are known for being served in stacks by waiters who expertly balance multiple plates on their arms.
